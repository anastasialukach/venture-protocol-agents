## Executive Summary

All existing runway calculators assume traditional software development — hiring human developers at market rates. This creates a massive gap: none account for AI agents, no-code tools, or the 4x productivity multiplier available to modern founders.

## Market Opportunity

### Keyword Data
- "startup runway calculator" — high search intent
- Low SEO difficulty — opportunity to rank quickly
- Growing search volume year-over-year

### Market Trends
- 70% of enterprise apps will use low-code/no-code by 2026 (Gartner)
- AI agent pricing: $2,000-5,000/month vs $15,000+/month for developers
- 4x faster development speed reported by AI-first startups

## Competitor Analysis

10 runway calculators analyzed. Key findings:
- All assume human hiring costs
- None compare traditional vs AI-accelerated development
- No tool recommendations based on project scope
- No consideration of no-code/low-code alternatives

## Recommendation

Build AI Runway Calculator MVP that shows founders:
1. Traditional runway (human-only team)
2. AI-accelerated runway (1-2 AI orchestrators + tools)
3. Cost savings
4. Recommended tool stack by project scope
